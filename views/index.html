> What the BodyNiser is?

The BodyNiser is an application developed for iOS or Android systems, which contains many useful functions, intended to become your personal assistant in your sport trainings, exercises, progress records keeping, new friends meetings and many many more...

To get more valuable information , please view the tutorial video about the BodyNizer.

Our Users

> Who can use the BodyNiser?

The BodyNiser is successfully used by many peoples around the World, who go in for sport, fitness and bodybuilding trainings and wish to have own small but powerful assistant like BodyNizer for each day to improve self achievements and attain the new horizons together.

This is a good time for join us!

Benefits from BodyNizer

> What benefits can I get from BodyNiser?

As we mentioned before, BodyNizer contains a lot of useful functions collected in one application, most valuable are:
- Big number of programs for different types of trainings.
- Recordind your progress, showing your statistic of trainings and advise how to make your trainings more effectively.
- If you are an advanced sportsmen, you can create your own training programs in the Builder and sale it on the Market.

The list is much longer, but better try the BodyNizer yourself, to make all of this benefits yours!
